Turn Steps

    Start phase

    1. Draw 2 cards
    2. Translate the cards in your hand

    Main phase

    1. Play up to 1 character
    2. Play up to 2 episodes
    3. Play as many events as you want

    Approach phase

    1. Attack the Futaro cards with your characters (As many as you want to/can)
    2. Go back and forth with your opponent to take the lane

    End phase
