How to play

Quints is a back and forth card game where you want to be the first person to get 3 cards in your bride zone

There are 5 zones you can play your cards in

And there are 3 types of cards

  • Characters
  • Episodes
  • Events

You can play 1 Character and 2 episodes each turn, and as many events as you are able to

You get a bride in your bride zone by capturing one of the Futaro cards, "Get yo man" as Luke says

To capture a Futaro card you must match or beat the power of the Futaro card

Once you have captured a Futaro card, you put the character you attacked with into the bride zone, while discarding all attached cards, and do the effect of the Futaro card

When you attack a Futaro card, your opponent has an opportunity to play events from their hand to weaken your attack. You can play events in responce to this and this goes on until someone wins or loses the lane

You can attack more than one Futaro card per turn, so your opponent has to choose the right ones to stop
